Bruce's Beakers

05:00



How To Play

Imagine you are Bruce Willis in Die Hard 3.

There is a ticking bomb and the only way to defuse it is by placing exactly 4 litres of water on a sensor. However, you only have a tap with running water, a 3 litre beaker and a 5 litre beaker on hand.

There is only 5 minutes before the bomb goes off. Use the buttons to fill, empty or transfer water between the beakers.

You win the game when the larger beaker only has 4 litres within it


You Win!

What a legend. You solved the puzzle, defused the bomb and saved thousands of lives.


Game Over

You weren't able to complete the task. The bomb has exploded.
